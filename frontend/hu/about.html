Rólunk AI & Poem

AI & Poem is a web service that utilizes the latest artificial intelligence technology to allow anyone to easily and quickly create poems and listen to them in a beautiful voice.

Beyond simply generating text, we have combined high-quality speech synthesis (TTS) technology to deliver the emotion of poetry through sound.

Our Mission

"Everyone can be a poet."
We dream of a world where technology lowers the barriers to creation, and people around the world communicate and empathize through poetry across language barriers.

Technical Features

1. Large Language Model (LLM)
AI, which has learned millions of literary works, analyzes the user's input topic and creates original and emotional poems in real-time.

2. Multilingual Support
We support over 20 languages including Korean, English, Japanese, Chinese, and French, allowing global users to enjoy poetry in their native language.

3. Emotional Voice Reading
The AI reads the poem with a tone and speed that matches the atmosphere of the generated poem. Experience the emotion of poetry heard with your ears, beyond reading with your eyes.
